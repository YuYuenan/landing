Spirited Away

A Thousand and One is an animated film produced by Studio Ghibli and directed by Hayao Miyazaki, with a main voice cast of Shu Ruohai, Freedom Irisa, Akio Nakamura and Mari Natsuki. The film tells the story of Chihiro, who accidentally arrives in the world of the gods and goes through many trials and tribulations in order to save his family, who have been punished by being turned into pigs.Originally released in Japan on 20 July 2001 by distributor Toho, the film received universal acclaim, grossing $395 million at the end of the year. Originally released in Japan on 20 July 2001 by distributor Toho, the film received universal acclaim, grossing $395.8 million at the worldwide box office.
